2.1.1 NVC Assignment

Two methods are available for assigning NVC

  1. An R implementation of the Modular Analysis of Vegetation Information System (MAVIS). This method uses the Czekanowski index (Bloom 1981) to quantify the similarity between NVC survey data and the NVC Floristic Tables.
  2. An R implementation of the method outlined in Tipping et al. (2013). This method calculates the Jaccard similarity between user-inputted sample quadrats and pseudo-quadrats. This approach is noted as improving the accuracy of fit relative to the Czekanowski index for sample quadrats with poor species richness (Tipping et al. 2013).

2.1.2 Habitat Correspondences

Habitat correspondence data is drawn from the Joint Nature Conservation Committee (JNCC) Spreadsheet of Habitat Correspondences (JNCC 2008).

Currently, habitat correspondence data for UKHab is drawn from UKHab V1.1.

  • Cover-weighted Hill-Ellenberg:
    • Nitrogen Score (N)
    • Moisture Score (F)
    • Reaction Score (R)
    • Salinity Score (S)
    • Light Score (L)
